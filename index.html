The 2019 Maker Conference

Making an Accessible Future

Join is on November 1st-3rd to learn, make, create and collaborate. The theme of our 2019 Maker Conference is Making an Accessible Future.
Participant in the conference will work in teams to solve accessibility problems identified by a number of accessibility organizations. This Maker Conference offer participants the chance to learn new skills, interact with other makers and meet experts and attend hands-on sessions. Help us make an accessible future!
