The University of Ottawa MakerCon organizing committee is made up a group of dedicated students from the University of Ottawa who want to promote project based collaborative learning, innovative design and higher technical education.

Our Initiative

We aim to provide attendees opportunities for hands on learning, collaboration and networking. The conference will take place from November 1st to November 3rd, 2019 in our Nationâ€™s Capital. Projected attendance is estimated to be 150 people, with our main demographics to include students, makers, educators and entrepreneurs from around the world.

Why Sponsor Us?

The Makercon will be an excellent opportunity to support our initiative of making an accessible future. Makercon will enable attendees learn new skills and work with others to develop solutions to accessibility problems over the weekend. It is also an opportunity for organizations to market to makers. Makers are students or members of the public who like to build, design and tinker. Workshops will be provided to teach new skills and mentorship will be a key part of the weekend. The event ends with prototypes demonstrations and winners are announced on Sunday.
