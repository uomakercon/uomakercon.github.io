Useful information for attendees
OMMIC 2017 is coming up this week and we wanted to share some more details. We encourage you to come to the Gala on Friday. It's the opening ceremony for the conference, eat some great food, get to know the speakers and learn about makerspaces across the world. However if you have purchased a ticket and can no longer come, please let us know. 

When: 

  • Gala: Friday, October 27th: 5 - 9 pm 
  • Conference: 
    • Saturday, October 28th: 9 am to 5 pm 
    • Sunday, October 29th: 9 am to 4 pm
Where:
  • Gala: Media Centre, Morisset Hall, 1st floor, 65 University Private
  • Conference:
    • Saturday Registration: Minto Sports Complex (801 King Edward Ave)
    • Sunday Registration: SITE Kiosk (800 King Edward Ave)

Parking: 
  • Here is a link to all parking lots available on uOttawa campus, on Saturday and Sunday the closest parking lot is the Minto Sports Complex
Food: 
  • Gala: Afghani buffet with firnee dessert and assorted pastries
  • Saturday: 
    • Morning: Coffee and snacks
    • Lunch: Buffet taco bar with corn chips and guacamole
  • Sunday:
    • Morning: Coffee and snacks
    • Lunch: Sandwiches with assorted vegetables and dips
