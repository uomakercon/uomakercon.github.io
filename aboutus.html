About Us

MakerCon is our new re-branded OMMIC conference. At MakerCon 2019, we are trying something new that allows makers, students and educators to be more creative. This year we have partnered with a number of community partners who provided specific asks and your role through the weekend is to make a prototype that solves their needs. Join us to learn, make and get inspired.
This 2.5 days event is a student initiative supported by the University of Ottawa Faculty of Engineering and The Centre for Entrepreneurship and Engineering Design (CEED).
